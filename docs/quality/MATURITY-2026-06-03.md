## Top risks (blocking production-readiness)

1. **CI red on every recent run (0/15).** A gating-step bug (`Set lint result` in the external `inovacc/workflows` reusable workflow) aborts before tests/coverage/govulncheck execute, so the cmderr-90%, dual-coverage, and golden-smoke gates are all silently skipped. No green baseline = no trustworthy quality signal; new regressions are invisible. This alone caps the project at beta.
2. **Reachable Critical CVEs + no live vuln gate.** `docs/quality/vulnerabilities.md` lists 3 reachable Critical + 5 major Go stdlib/`x/net` CVEs (e.g. GO-2026-4918 HTTP/2 infinite loop) unpatched; `govulncheck` is documented as a CI gate but missing from `.github/workflows/test.yml`. No gitleaks/gosec/CodeQL or dependency-update automation.
3. **Versioning overclaims stability.** `v1.0.0–v1.5.0` tags imply a frozen API, yet 17 `pkg/*` packages are marked `// Experimental:`, the roadmap is mid-way to a *separate* "v1.0" supply-chain milestone, and binaries carry no version ldflags (HEAD is 174 untagged commits past v1.5.0). Plus: aggregate coverage is unmeasured and the entire cloud surface (aws/kubectl/vault/video) has zero unit tests.
